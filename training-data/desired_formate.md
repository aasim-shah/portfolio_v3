# Platform Overview & Introduction

## What is FyreWay?

FyreWay is a managed VPN infrastructure platform that enables companies to launch production-ready VPN services in hours instead of months. We handle all the complex backend infrastructure, allowing you to focus on building your VPN product or application.

## The Core Problem We Solve

Building VPN infrastructure from scratch typically takes 6-18 months and requires:
- Network architecture design and protocol decisions
- Global provider sourcing and negotiations
- Scaling logic and failure handling systems
- Monitoring, alerting, and reporting infrastructure
- Security hardening and compliance work
- Ongoing operational staffing and DevOps expertise

**FyreWay compresses this entire process into hours.**
