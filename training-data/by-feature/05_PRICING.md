## Pricing FAQs

**Q: Can I pay in installments?**
Yes! Projects 6+ months can be paid monthly. Otherwise, payment follows milestone schedule (30/40/30).

**Q: What if my budget is limited?**
We can phase the project or reduce scope to fit your budget. I'll recommend the best approach to maximize value within your constraints.

**Q: Are there any hidden costs?**
No. All costs are outlined in the proposal. Infrastructure costs (hosting, domains) are clearly separated from development costs.

**Q: Can I get a discount for a large project?**
Possibly. Contact me with your project details and budget for a custom quote. Long-term contracts often receive better rates.

**Q: What if the project takes longer than estimated?**
Fixed-price projects are honored regardless of time. Hourly projects may exceed estimates if scope expands — I always communicate this before additional charges.

**Q: Do you require contracts?**
For projects over $1,000, yes. A simple service agreement outlines scope, timeline, payment terms, IP rights, and cancellation policy.
