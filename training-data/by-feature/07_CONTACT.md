## Booking Process Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Initial Contact (Day 1)
- Send email, use contact form, click "Schedule Meeting," or call during business hours
- Receive acknowledgment within 24 hours
- Initial questions may be asked, meeting scheduled if needed

### Step 2: Free Consultation (Day 2–3)
**Duration:** 15–60 minutes (depending on complexity)
**Format:** Video call (Google Meet/Zoom) or phone

**Discussion Topics:**
- Your business and goals
- Project requirements
- Target audience
- Timeline expectations
- Budget considerations
- Technical questions
- Similar project examples

**What You Should Prepare:**
- Project overview document
- Reference websites/apps (if any)
- List of required features
- Timeline expectations
- Questions for me

**What I Provide:**
- Initial feasibility assessment
- Technology recommendations
- Rough timeline estimate
- Approximate cost range
- Next steps explanation

### Step 3: Proposal Delivery (Day 4–7)
**Timeline:** 2–3 business days after consultation
**Delivered via:** Email (PDF format)

**Proposal Includes:**
- Executive summary
- Complete scope of work
- Feature breakdown
- Technology stack recommendation
- Milestone timeline
- Detailed pricing (fixed or hourly)
- Payment schedule
- Deliverables list
- Terms and conditions
- Acceptance form

### Step 4: Review & Discussion (Day 8–10)
- Review proposal thoroughly
- Note any questions or concerns
- Discuss changes if needed
- Request clarifications
- Adjustments made as necessary

### Step 5: Agreement (Day 11–14)
For projects over $1,000, a simple service agreement covers:
- Scope of work (detailed)
- Timeline and milestones
- Payment terms
- Intellectual property rights
- Confidentiality clauses
- Cancellation/refund policy
- Dispute resolution
- Governing law

### Step 6: Initial Payment (Day 14–15)
**Deposit Required:** 30% (standard)

After payment:
- Receipt issued immediately
- Project officially begins
- Kickoff meeting scheduled
- Project management access provided

### Step 7: Project Kickoff (Day 15–16)
**Duration:** 60–90 minutes via video call

**Agenda:**
- Confirm all requirements
- Review timeline and milestones
- Establish communication channels
- Set up project management tools (Trello/Asana)
- Discuss feedback process
- Share initial resources

### Step 8: Development Begins (Day 17+)
**Regular Updates:**
- Weekly progress emails
- Milestone demonstrations
- Open communication channels
- Regular video check-ins
